# Medical terminology and common medical acronyms

## Word stems
- **adeno**. glandular tissue
- **carcin**. cancerous
- **noma**. unusual tissue growth
- **pneumo**. air
- **thorax**. chest
- **chole**. gall bladder/bile
- **docho**. duct
- **lith**. stone

## Abbreviations
- **CAD**. coronary artery disease
- **CHF**. congestive heart failure
- **STEMI**. ST segment elevation myocardial infarction

### Common labs
- **BMP**. basic metabolic panel
  - Na, K, Cl, Bicarb, BUN, Cr, Glu
- **LFT**. liver function tests
- **Coags** coagulation tests
