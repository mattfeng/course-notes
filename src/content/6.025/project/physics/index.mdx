### Properties
- **1x TBE buffer**
  - $\sigma = \pu{[0.5, 1.5] mS/cm}$
  - $\rho = \pu{[667, 1000] \ohm cm}$
  - $D = \pu{1.01 g/cm^3}$

### Calculations
- Variables
  - $V$. voltage
    - $V \in \pu{[120, 4000] V}$
  - $P$. power
  - $I$. current
  - $R$. resistance
  - $\sigma$. conductivity
  - $\rho$. resistivity
    - $\rho = \pu{10^{15} \ohm m} = \pu{10^{17} \ohm cm}$
  - $\Cp$. specific heat
    - $\Cp = \pu{725 J/kg K} = \pu{0.725 J/g k}$
  - $D$. density
    - $D = \pu{2.17 g/cm^3} = \pu{2.17 Mg/m^3}$
  - $q$. heat
- $V = IR$
- $P = IV = V^2/R$
- $R = \rho \frac{L}{A}$
  - $L$. length
  - $A$. cross sectional area
- $q = m\Cp \DT = Pt$
  - $t$. migration time
- Combine all equations to get
  - $\boxed{\DT = \dfrac{V^2t}{\rho D L^2 \Cp}}$
  - For 40 minutes at 4 kV, 10cm capillary
    - $\DT = \frac{(4000^2)(40 \times 60)}{(10^{17})(2.17)(10)^2(0.725)} = \pu{2.44 \times 10^{-9} K}$, **negligible heating**

## Mobility
- **ion velocity**. $$v = \mu_e E$$
  - $v$. ion velocity
  - $E$. electric field strength
  - $\mu_e$. electrophoretic mobility
- **electrophoretic mobility (for a spherical ion)**. $$\mu_e = \dfrac{q}{6\pi\eta r}$$
  - $\mu_e$. electrophoretic mobility
  - $q$. ion charge
  - $\eta$. **solution viscosity**
  - $r$. ion radius
