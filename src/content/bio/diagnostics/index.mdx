# diagnostics

## lateral flow assays

- samples
    - urine
    - saliva
    - sweat
    - serum
    - plasma
- screen for
    - pathogens
    - chemicals
    - toxins
    - pollutants
    - animal diseases
    - antigens and antibodies
- LFAs have long shelf life and do not require refrigeration
- **lateral flow immunoassay (LFIA).** uses antibodies exclusively as recognition elements

- **control line.** indicates that liquid actually flowed
- direct vs competitive
    - direct: test line = positive result
    - competitive: no test line = positive result
