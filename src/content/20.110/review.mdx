- micelles _form a set size and shape_, and not a distribution
- effectively a hill equation

<Collapse title="Energetic and entropic factors favoring micellization">

- Energetically disfavored (water-hydrophobic tail interactions instead of
water/water and hydrophobic/hydrophobic interactions)
    </td>
    <td>

- Translational entropy loss for 1 micelle vs. 60 (or whatever number) individual
molecules
- Energy gain for buying hydrophobics into a core
- Size and shape controlled by size and shape of detergent
