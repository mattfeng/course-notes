# glossary (math)

- **dependent variables.**
    - also known as: **responses**, **outputs**
- **independent variables.**
    - also known as: **features**, **inputs**, **predictors**
- **qualitative variable.**
    - also known as: **factors**, **categorical variables**, **discrete variables**
- **ordered categorical variable.** an ordering exists between values, but no metric notion is appropriate
- **targets.** binary numerical codes for qualitative variables.
- **dummy variables.** $K$ binary variables, with only one "on" at a time, one for each possible class.
