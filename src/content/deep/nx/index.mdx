```python
nx.draw(G, pos=nx.nx_agraph.graphviz_layout(G, prog="neato"))
```

- [`networkx.drawing.nx_agraph.graphviz_layout(G, prog="neato")`](https://networkx.org/documentation/stable/reference/generated/networkx.drawing.nx_agraph.graphviz_layout.html)
    - Other programs
        - `dot`. hierarchical drawings of directed graphs
        - `neato`. spring model, reduces total energy
        - `fdp`. spring model, reduces forces, not as good
        - `sfdp`. spring model, reduces forces, not as good, scales for large graphs
        - `twopi`. radial
        - `circo`.
