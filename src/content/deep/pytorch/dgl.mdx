    dgl.data.CoraGraphDataset(),

    dgl.data.QM9EdgeDataset()
]

### [QM9Dataset](https://docs.dgl.ai/en/0.6.x/api/python/dgl.data.html#qm9-dataset)

- ndata
    - `X.ndata['R']`. cartesian coordinate
    - `X.ndata['Z']`. atomic number
    - `X.nodes()`. get nodes
    - `X.edges()`. get edges
